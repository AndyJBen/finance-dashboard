Frequently Asked Questions

Have questions? We've got answers. Find information about our services, process, and policies below.

What areas do you serve? -

We primarily serve the Greater Springfield area, including Centerville, Oakton, and surrounding counties within a 50-mile radius. Please contact us to confirm if we cover your specific location.

Are you licensed and insured? +

Yes, WorkForce Pro is fully licensed and insured for all the services we offer. We carry comprehensive liability insurance and workers' compensation coverage for your peace of mind and our team's protection.

How do I get a quote for my project? +

You can request a free, no-obligation quote by filling out the contact form on our website, calling us directly, or sending us an email. We typically schedule a site visit for larger projects to provide the most accurate estimate.

What are your payment terms? +

Payment terms vary depending on the project size and duration. For smaller jobs, payment is typically due upon completion. For larger projects, we may require a deposit upfront with progress payments scheduled throughout the project. We accept checks, credit cards, and electronic bank transfers.
